subquery can be a additional query statement in either
the select statement,
the from statement (subquery must have an alias),
the join statement,
the where statement,

WHERE clause
Single Value: >, <, >=, <=, =, <>, !=
Single column: IN, NOT IN, (operator) (ALL | SOME | ANY)

correlated subqueries is a subquery that refers to a column of a table that is not in its FROM clause.
